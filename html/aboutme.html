Lance Higdon Location: Midwest, USA Family: Proud father of two children and owner of two adorable dogs About Lance Higdon: Lance Higdon is a Midwest native who has a deep love for the great outdoors and an adventurous spirit. Personal Life: When Lance is not out and about, he cherishes spending quality time with his two wonderful children and enjoys making precious memories with them. Whether it's engaging in outdoor activities together or simply having fun indoors, family holds a special place in Lance's heart. Lance Higdon embodies a zest for life and a true appreciation for the beauty of nature. With a remarkable background in the military, a love for outdoor adventures, and a commitment to fitness, Lance embraces each day as an opportunity for new experiences and personal growth.
